ABOUT US

Inspireel Public School is designed to offer children with a healthy atmosphere in which to develop cognitively, intellectually, artistically, and athletically, as well as to instill our moral and cultural values, allowing them to become responsible global citizens of the future. We are developing a curriculum and pedagogy for Early Childhood Care and Education with a 20:1 ratio, focusing on flexible, multilevel play/activity-based learning over six years.
